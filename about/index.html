About

Isaac S. Crum

Isaac is a technology and intellectual property attorney who helps clients resolve disputes, protect brands and technology, and make practical decisions about IP assets.

Experience

Isaac has more than 15 years of experience representing clients in intellectual property and technology matters. His work has included patent, trademark, copyright, trade secret, unfair competition, licensing, and technology-related litigation and counseling.

He has litigated cases in federal district courts around the country and has handled matters before the United States Patent and Trademark Office, the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board, the International Trade Commission, and appellate courts.

Isaac’s background in computer engineering helps him work efficiently with technical teams, understand product and software issues, and translate complex technology into clear advocacy and practical advice.

Before launching Crum IP, Isaac practiced at national and international law firms and represented clients ranging from startups and small businesses to large technology companies.

Education and technical background

Isaac earned his computer engineering degree from the University of Arizona and attended law school at the University of Arizona. His practice combines litigation judgment, IP strategy, and technical fluency.
